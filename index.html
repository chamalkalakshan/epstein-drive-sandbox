Controls and Help

Mouse Controls

ClickSelect origin body (first click) or destination (second click)
ScrollZoom in and out on the solar system map
Mid dragPan the map by holding middle mouse button and dragging
R-dragPan the map by holding right mouse button and dragging
HoverShow body name, faction, and distance from origin

Keyboard Shortcuts

SpaceToggle orbit animation (planets move at real relative speeds)
FStart or stop the flight simulation along the current route
EscClear the current route and reset origin and destination
RReset zoom to default and re-center the map
HOpen or close this help panel

How to Plan a Route

1Click any body in the left list or directly on the map to set your ORIGIN (highlighted in amber)
2Click a second body to set your DESTINATION (highlighted in red). The trajectory arc appears immediately.
3Read the flight plan in the right panel: travel time, peak velocity, fuel budget, and crew experience.
4Press F or click Simulate Flight to watch the ship travel along the route with an engine plume.
5Add waypoints with the Add Waypoint button to plan multi-leg journeys (up to 3 legs).
6Click Share Route to copy a URL that restores this exact route and ship config in any browser.

Header Toggles

OrbitsAnimate planets orbiting the sun at real relative speeds
EventsShow Expanse lore markers: Eros incident, Ganymede, Ring Gate activation
FactionsOverlay UN, MCRN, OPA Belt, and outer planet territory zones
Rel. FXShow proper ship clock time alongside coordinate time using Lorentz factor

Ship Parameters (Left Panel)

ThrustAcceleration in g-force. 0.33g is Rocinante cruise. Above 3g requires Juice.
IspSpecific impulse in seconds. Higher = more efficient engine. Epstein drives: 30,000-60,000s.
Fuel RatioWet mass / dry mass. Higher ratio means more propellant and longer range.
CargoExtra payload mass in tonnes. Increases fuel needed and travel time.
Tip: use the Year slider or enable Orbit Animation to find the closest approach between two bodies, then click "Jump to Optimal Year" in the flight plan panel to snap to the best launch window. Use the Share Route button to save and share any configuration as a URL.
